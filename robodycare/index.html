Robodycare

Mind-Controlled Healthcare System with Biofeedback

Created for Big Ideas at Berkeley

Hello There

RobodyCare uses machine learning and efficient algorithms to improve people's mental and physical health. The mind-controlled massager uses EEG brain waves, 3D printing and embedded systems to help both diagnosis and treatment of stress and chronic pain. It exemplifies the democratization of data-driven healthcare methods at low cost.

Hardware Composition


NeuroSky EEG Brainwave Reader

Interpreted with machine learning

Vibrational Motors

Controlled by Raspberry Pi with Pulse-Width-Modulation

3D Printed Motor Mounts


EEG, Raspberry Pi, Machine Learning, 3D Printing, Laser Cutting
